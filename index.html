Olive oil extraction

We make olive oil for you characterized by its pure green color, rich flavor and health benefits. It is obtained from the best olives, which gives it a unique taste and Real Purity. The secret of its quality lies in the method of production mastered in all its details, which makes it an irreplaceable addition in the kitchen and a right option for everyday occasions.

Types of olives

We have Olives of various types presented to you in all its flavors, carrying its many benefits from an immunostimulant and a fighter against cardiovascular diseases to regulating blood sugar.

olive bagasse

We provide you with wet Olive Cake, which are semi-solid by-products with a strong aroma and a doughy texture known as peat or olive heaviness. It can be used for heating, cooking or making charcoal because it stays burning longer. It can also be used to make animal feed because it is rich in fiber.
